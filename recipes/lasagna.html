Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish renowned for its layered structure and rich, savory flavors. Typically, it consists of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, a robust meat or vegetable ragù, creamy béchamel sauce, and generous amounts of cheese, often including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. The dish is assembled in a deep baking dish and cooked until the pasta is tender, the sauces meld harmoniously, and the top is golden and bubbling. Lasagna is celebrated for its versatility, with numerous regional and contemporary variations that incorporate diverse ingredients and spices, making it a beloved staple in both Italian cuisine and global culinary practices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (wide, flat pasta sheets)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (or a combination)
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Fresh basil (chopped)
  • Fennel seeds
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and cook until the onion is tender.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and water. Mix well.
  5. Season with sugar, fresh basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and fresh parsley. Mix until well blended.
  7. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  8. Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Arrange 6 cooked lasagna noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce.
  10. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  11. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese and 1/3 of the Parmesan cheese.
  12. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  13. Finish with a final layer of noodles, remaining meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  14. Cover with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray foil with cooking spray).
  15. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 25 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  16. Cool for 15 minutes before serving to allow the lasagna to set.
